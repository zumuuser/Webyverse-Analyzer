help_outline Understanding Your Results

Each phone lookup provides comprehensive analysis across multiple validation criteria. Here's what each metric means:

check_circle Validity Status
Valid: Phone number is correctly formatted and likely exists.
Invalid: Phone number is incorrectly formatted or does not exist.
score Confidence Score (0-100)
80-100: High confidence, reliable data.
60-79: Good confidence, minor uncertainties.
40-59: Moderate confidence, some data gaps.
0-39: Low confidence, significant data gaps or issues.
public Country & Location
Identifies the country and general geographic location associated with the phone number.
cell_tower Carrier Information
Detects the mobile or landline carrier (e.g., AT&T, Vodafone) and the type of line.
schedule Timezone Information
Determines the timezone of the phone number, including current time and UTC offset.
security Data Breach Check
Scans for instances where the phone number may have appeared in known data breaches.
